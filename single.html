Bugiri Chief Magistrate's Court Sees Arraignment of Six Individuals on Corruption Charges On September 9, 2024, the State House Anti-Corruption Unit and Criminal Investigations Department presented six defendants before the Chief Magistrate's Court in Bugiri. The accused, comprising local government officials and PDM SACCO leaders, face charges of Obtaining Money by False Pretense and Conspiracy to Defraud. The defendants are Mugunda Azedy, Assistant Town Clerk and Community Development Officer, Busowa Town Council, Maka Christopher, Parish Chief, Buwanga Sub County, Busowa Parish , Kawudhu Mathias, Chairperson, Nawandhuki Busowa SACCa , Balidawa Stephen, Chairperson, Nakidudula Busowa SACCO, Makiika Stephen, Chairperson, Nakawa Busowa SACCO, Waiswa Hassan, Businessman The court remanded the accused until September 23, 2024.

Bugisu Cooperative Union (BCU)

Bugisu Cooperative Union (BCU) has announced new coffee prices ahead of the upcoming season. BCU has set the price at 12,000 Uganda Shillings per kilogram for premium coffee and 11,500 Uganda Shillings for G1 coffee. This is inclusive of transport and commission costs. BCU Board Chairperson, Nathan Nandala Mafabi has urged farmers to avoid selling their coffee to multinational companies, accusing them of exploiting farmers. According to Nandala, if all Bamasaba farmers supplied their coffee to BCU, their needs would be met in an efficient and timely manner. BCU board treasurer, Nathan Mabonga said that they are committed to maintain competitive coffee prices and improving the payment system through instant cash for coffee delivered.

Police in Kiira Region Arrest Suspect for Impersonation

Police in Kiira Region Arrest Suspect for Impersonation and Theft Police in Kiira Region have arrested a suspect, Jackson Mukasa, aged 40, also known as Afande CAO, who claimed to be working with the Field Force Unit (FFU). Mukasa is charged with stealing 700,000 shillings and an Infinix Hot 8 phone, and for requesting money from victims under the pretext of tracking their stolen property on behalf of the police The suspect was arrested up in masese landing site in masese walukuba 11th September 2024 while hiding up and taken to jinja central police station where he is detained now. According to SP James Mubi, Kiira Region Police Spokesperson confirmed the arrest and says, on August 8th, 2024, Mukasa stole a phone and 700,000 shillings from a chapati seller and later requested additional money to "track" the stolen property.
